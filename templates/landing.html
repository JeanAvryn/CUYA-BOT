About CUYA-BOT

CUYA-BOT is a disaster mitigation chatbot specifically designed for Cuyapo, Nueva Ecija. It helps citizens report emergencies, connect with government units, and access real-time safety updates. It was developed by STEM students of Dr. Ramon de Santos National High School, Cuyapo, Nueva Ecija to further improve and speed up disaster mitigation in their town.
